Hibernate自动生成表遇到的问题Caused by: java.sql.SQLException: ORA-01747:

问题：Caused by: java.sql.SQLException: ORA-01747: invalid user.table.column, table.co
lumn, or column specification

原因：*。hbm.xml文件中的column名称与Oracle的关键字冲突。

<property name="time" type="string">
            <column name="TIME" length="19" not-null="true">
                <comment></comment>
            </column>
        </property>

修改为： <property name="time" type="string">
            <column name="ALERT_TIME" length="19" not-null="true">
                <comment></comment>
            </column>
        </property>
